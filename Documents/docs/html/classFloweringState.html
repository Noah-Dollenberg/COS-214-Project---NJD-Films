Detailed Description

Represents the flowering state in a plant's lifecycle.

The flowering state is when plants produce blooms. This is often the peak of the plant's visual appeal and requires careful maintenance to prolong the flowering period. After flowering completes, plants may enter DormantState or cycle back depending on the species.
